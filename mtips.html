Tips

Here are some tips about how to order effectively from our restaurant:

  1. Menu Exploration: Ask our staff for recommendations if you're unsure of what to order.
  2. Dietary Restrictions: Let us know if you have any allergies or dietary restrictions, and we will happily accommodate!
  3. Seasonal Specials: Check out our seasonal specials for dishes that highlight fresh, in-season ingredients.
  4. Reheating: For leftovers, most dishes will reheat well in the microwave, but reheating stir-fried dishes on a stovetop has the best results.
  5. Happy Hour Deals: When placing an order over the phone, ask our employees about special discounts during happy hour or lunchtime specials.
  6. Loyalty Program: Sign up for our loyalty program for discounts and special promotions!
